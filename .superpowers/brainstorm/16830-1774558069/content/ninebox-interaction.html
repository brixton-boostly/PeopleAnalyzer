How should managers place employees on the 9-box?

Choose the interaction style that feels most natural

A

Drag & Drop

Employee names appear in a sidebar. Manager drags each person into the correct cell on the grid.

  • Most intuitive for spatial thinking
  • More complex to build
  • Works best on desktop
B

Click to Place

Manager clicks an employee in the sidebar to select them, then clicks a cell on the grid to place them there.

  • Simple and reliable
  • Works on all devices
  • Clear two-step action
C

Dropdown per Employee

Each employee in the list has two dropdowns — one for Performance, one for Potential. No grid interaction needed.

  • Simplest to build & use
  • Less visual / spatial feel
  • Grid shown as read-only summary
